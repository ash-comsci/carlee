Support For Non-Traditional Career Paths

Society for Canadian Women in Science & Technology

According to their website, the Society for Canadian Women in Science & Technology (SCWIST) is ‘a not-for-profit society that specializes in improving the presence and influence of women and girls in STEM (...). SCWIST promotes participation and advancement through education, networking, mentorship, collaborative partnerships and advocacy.’ It offers mentorship programs and a supportive community, for women looking to enter computer science as a field of study. Students can join SCWIST by becoming a member. For more information, click here.

Girls sySTEM

Girls in sySTEM wants to increase the number of women in the field. It is a mentorship program, where girls get to connect with people who can inspire them to pursue the field. The Girls in sySTEM program offers a supportive community for girls in STEM, a mentorship program, and even scholarship opportunities to help girls succeed in the field postsecondary. Students can join sySTEM by joining the program and becoming a mentor or mentee. For more information, click here.
